# FAQ

### Do I have to be registered for the main conference to participate at the workshop?
Yes. Registration to the main conference includes all workshops, alternatively you may register only for the workshops. Please register here [here](https://icml.cc/Register2). 

### Can I submit published work?
Yes. Any submission that was previously published (except at ICML 2026) or is under review can be submitted. Please use our style files for submission (see [Call for Papers](cfp) for details).
