<p><strong>Biography:</strong> Sidak Pal Singh is a Research Scientist at Google DeepMind whose research investigates weight-space symmetries and their role in model merging. His research develops principled alignment methods based on optimal transport and permutation symmetries, enabling effective merging by accounting for symmetry-induced redundancies in parameter space. His recent work extends linear mode connectivity to transformers, demonstrating how symmetry-aware alignment enables merging and interpolation of large-scale foundation models.</p>

<p><strong>Biography:</strong> Robin Walters is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Northeastern University and the director of the Geometric Learning Lab.
